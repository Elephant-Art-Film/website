Join the **ELEPHANT crew** on **November 29, 2025** for an inspiring evening of cinema at the **Cinema Hall of Center Zalog** in Ljubljana. This year’s program brings together three short films by director Jan Fabris, each offering a unique and immersive storytelling experience. All screenings will include English subtitles to welcome viewers from around the world.
The evening will open with a live piano performance by Ruslan Perezhilo, bringing a powerful selection of cinematic music to set the mood.

## Featured Films:

- Blue Bunny – A woman, struggling with depression, anxiety and a sense of guilt, finds a new life.
- [Ten Till Seven](/Ten-Till-Seven) – The content of the letter is slowly revealing, but some secrets do not permit themselves to be told.
- [Lev Berger](/Lev-Berger) – When the sound recordist realises that their TV report involving the country’s most popular politician has been faked, he sets out to find the truth.

## Event Details:

**Date:** 29th of November, 2025  
**Time:** 19:00 CET (7:00 PM)  
**Venue:** [Cinema Hall of Center Zalog, Zaloška cesta 267, Ljubljana](https://maps.app.goo.gl/1fBvR7ZYgGKg69GF9)
