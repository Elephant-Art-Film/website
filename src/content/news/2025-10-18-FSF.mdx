We are proud to announce that our short film Lev Berger, directed by Jan Fabris, has been officially selected for the competition Programme at the 28th Festival of Slovenian Film (FSF) in Portorož.

#### Screening details:
- Date: Friday, 24th October 2025
- Time: 14:45
- Location: MAGAZIN GRANDO
- Section: Tekmovalni kratki III

We extend our heartfelt congratulations to the entire cast and crew for their exceptional work, dedication, and bringing this story to life.

We would also like to thank the festival jury and the FSF selection committee for including Lev Berger among this year’s competing short films. It’s an honor to share the screen alongside many inspiring works from across Slovenia.

We warmly invite everyone to join us in Portorož and watch Lev Berger on the big screen. Your presence and support mean a lot to us and to the talented team behind the film.

For more information about the festival and to purchase tickets, please visit: [FSF official website](https://fsf.si/2025/)
