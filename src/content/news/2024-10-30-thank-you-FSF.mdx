Thank you to the selectors for including three of our short films in this year’s Festival slovenskega filma (FSF), with two of them making it into the competition program. Voyeuress, Cold Blue, and Klara's Dance were all warmly received by audiences, journalists, and critics alike.

Heartfelt thanks to everyone for the beautiful memories, unforgettable moments, and the new connections we’ve made.
